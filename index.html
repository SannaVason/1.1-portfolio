About Me

It took a handful of years before I figured out where I was meant to go in life. I drifted in through different educations, good at all of them, but not excited for any of them. Then I attempted full stack development in a collegiate space, but quickly recoiled after my professor told me I would be an embarrassment to myself and those that taught me, because of my learning disabilities. A couple years later, I rediscovered coding through my spouse and friends who work in the game development industry and ended up thriving here at Thinkful.

Outside of a screen, I read books of all kinds and annotate them if possible. When I don't have my nose in a book, I've got my pen to a page writing poetry and short stories.
